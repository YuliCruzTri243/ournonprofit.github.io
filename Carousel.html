The Problem of Deforestation

Deforestation has egregious effects on our planet. As we continue to cut down trees the worst are planet's state becomes with climate change and our natural habitats. Every year 3.5 to 7 billion trees are cut down.

We Have a Mission to Teach Schools on how to Properly Recycle Textbooks and use the Technology they are Given.

Schools are often very misinformed on how to properly use and teach with the technology they have. As result, they have useless textbooks and computers that aren't being used to there full potential.
